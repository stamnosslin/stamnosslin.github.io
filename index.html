Express your uncertainty — assign probabilities, not just one answer.

How this test works

Instead of picking a single answer, you distribute 100% probability across all options — reflecting how confident you are in each one.

  • Use the sliders to assign % to each option
  • All options must sum to exactly 100%
  • Scoring formula: S = 25 × (0.8 − Brier score)
