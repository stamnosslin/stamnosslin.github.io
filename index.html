Probabilistic MCQ

Express your uncertainty — assign probabilities, not just one answer.

How this test works

Instead of picking a single answer, you distribute 100% probability across all options — reflecting how confident you are in each one.

  • Use the sliders to assign % to each option
  • All five options must sum to exactly 100%
  • You can auto-adjust or normalize manually
  • Your score per question ranges from 0 (worst) to 1 (best)

A perfect score means you put 100% on the correct answer.
Spreading probability wisely still earns partial credit.
